**CRTR IconButton** — icon-only control for socials, media transport, and corner actions; pairs with the brand's circular and squared marks.

```jsx
<IconButton aria-label="Instagram">IG</IconButton>
<IconButton variant="red" aria-label="Play"><PlayIcon/></IconButton>
<IconButton variant="solid" shape="square" aria-label="Close">✕</IconButton>
```

Variants: `outline` (paper → red on hover), `solid` (ink), `red`. Shapes: `circle` `square`. `outline` swaps to red on hover; `solid`/`red` lift with a hard shadow.
